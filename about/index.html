About this site

This website is currently hosted on GitHub Pages, generated by Hugo in my theme SakuraChiyo. I designed the theme applying a colour scheme in the way of Chiyo Sakura, a character in anime Monthly Girls’ Nozaki-kun.

It was difficult for me to develop theme SakuraChiyo. Knowing nothing about GO language, I had to seek quite a bit of assistance from DeepSeek during my first front-end development. By the way, I must say the official documents of Hugo are unfriendly organised. If you’d like to use my theme but have no interest in the character or the anime, you might improve the CSS to meet your demands.
